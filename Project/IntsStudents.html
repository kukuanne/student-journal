General Information

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

  1. Dully filled in application Form (downloadable here);
  2. Secondary school transcripts/reports for senior 4, 5 and senior 6 ;
  3. Two Passport size photos in color;
  4. A photocopy of the National ID Card or valid Passport;
  5. A proof/copy of health insurance certificate;
  6. A non-refundable application fee (50 USD for international applicants) paid in any of the following bank accounts:
  • Bank: 4003211197369 (FRWS)

  • Bank of Kigali: 00040 – 00280275 -75 (FRWS)

  • Bank of Kigali: 00040 – 00292172 – 41 (USD)

    • Swift Code: BKIGRWRW | IBAN: 40000400029217241003

NOTE:

  1. Applicants with foreign academic qualifications shall obtain an equivalence of their certificates from Rwanda Basic Education Board (REB).
  2. An entry Visa is granted upon arrival at the Kigali International Airport for all citizens from around the world.
  3. An international student will need apply for a study visa within two weeks (15 days) upon arrival. To do this, he/she will produce the following documents to the Rwanda’s Immigration Services: A Police Clearance from the home country; a valid passport, a CV and an application letter.

Graduate Admission Requirements

The following are requirements for admission into Masters programme:
  1. A well filled in application form (Downloadable here)
  2. Official receipt of application fee payment (50 USD for international applicants)
  3. Certified photocopies of college or university diplomas or degree certificates
  4. Two recent passport-size photos
  5. Three letters of recommendation from referees in sealed envelopes
  6. Two certified copies of the official transcript from each college/ university attended (certified by the institution or Commissioner of Oath)
  7. Certified photocopy of secondary school certificate
  8. Updated curriculum vitae (CV)
  9. A proof/copy of health insurance certificate
  10. Essay (Explaining educational goals, career objectives, work experience, awards, and/or extracurricular activities)
